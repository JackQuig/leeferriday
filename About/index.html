Lee Ferriday

Independent Education Consultant

Lee has been working as highly effective school improvement adviser for a decade. He has recently been appointed as a Consultant Head at a school in Telford in order to work with the staff in transformIng the quality of education.

Lee has allocated one day each week to continue his work in school improvement and welcomes contact from colleagues in other schools. He has enjoyed a highly successful 32 years in education, as a Headteacher he led a school to an Outstanding Ofsted judgement.

Lee has always been in demand in other areas of education. He has worked as learning and development adviser for an international infrastructure company and played a key role in establishing the Severn Teaching School Alliance of over 50 schools, where he was the Director of Operations and Initial Teacher Training.

He is dedicated to supporting the professional development of school staff, his training and course delivery has always been well received by school leaders, aspiring leaders, teachers, support staff and governors.

When not supporting schools Lee can be found walking hills, riding his bike, in the garden with his chickens and following his favourite football team.
